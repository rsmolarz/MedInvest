Introduction

This privacy policy describes how MedInvest ("MedInvest," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, shares, and otherwise handles personal information about individuals ("you" and/or "your") who visit and use our websites and mobile applications that include a link to this privacy policy, and any related products, features, and services (collectively, the "Service" or "Services").

MedInvest is a United States company. The MedInvest Service is designed specifically for healthcare professionals in the United States interested in investment education and professional networking. By using the Service, you consent to your personal information being stored and otherwise processed in the United States as described in this policy.

By using the Service, whether as a registered member or an unregistered visitor, you acknowledge that you have read this privacy policy, and consent to the handling of your personal information as described in this policy.

1. Collection of Information

We collect personal information that you provide us directly, from third party sources, and automatically when you use the Service as follows:

Information That You Provide To Us Directly

MedInvest Member Accounts

When you register a MedInvest account and become a member, we require you to provide certain personal information including your name, email address, medical specialty, and professional credentials. Registering users also have the option to provide additional information including subspecialty, clinical and professional interests, profile photo, hospital affiliations, and employment history.

User Content and Submissions

We collect and may retain content and information that you share with us including comments, posts, investment insights, survey responses, and communications with MedInvest user support.

Information Collected Automatically

We collect information automatically when you use the Services through the use of cookies and similar technologies. This includes activity information such as the date and time you access our sites, pages viewed, and your general location based on IP address.

Information From Third Party Sources

We may obtain information from third party sources, including professional associations and our business partners, to verify your professional credentials and enhance your experience.

2. Uses of Personal Information

We may use the personal information we collect about you for the following purposes:

  • Provision of Service: To provide, operate, support, and improve the Services.
  • Member Profiles: To create and maintain your member profile and account.
  • Communications: To communicate with you about the Service, including relevant content, events, and marketing.
  • Professional Connections: To suggest potential connections and help you network with other healthcare professionals.
  • Personalization: To personalize the Services including content, investment opportunities, and recommendations.
  • Security: To detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or harmful activities.

3. Sharing of Information

We may share your personal information with third parties as follows:

  • Public Profiles: Public profile information is viewable by other members unless you adjust your privacy settings.
  • Service Providers: We share information with service providers who assist us in providing the Services.
  • Business Partners: We may share information with trusted business partners for services that benefit our members.
  • Legal Requirements: We may share information when required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

4. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We use cookies and similar technologies to collect information about your use of our Services. Cookies help us serve you better by understanding your interests, tracking trends, and saving your preferences.

You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect your ability to use certain features of our Services.

5. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as necessary to provide the Services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

7. Your Choices

You have the following choices regarding your personal information:

  • Account Information: You may update or correct your account information at any time through your profile settings.
  • Marketing Communications: You may opt out of marketing communications by following the unsubscribe instructions in our emails.
  • Cookies: You can manage cookie preferences through your browser settings.
  • Account Deletion: You may request deletion of your account by contacting us.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.
