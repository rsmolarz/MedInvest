Op-MedInvest Submission Guidelines

Op-MedInvest publishes thoughtful essays from medical professionals about personal finance, investing, and wealth building. We're looking for authentic voices sharing real experiences and practical insights.

What We're Looking For

  • Personal investment journeys - Your path to financial independence
  • Specialty-specific insights - How your field affects your finances
  • Real estate experiences - Syndications, rentals, development deals
  • Market analysis - Your perspective on current opportunities
  • Retirement strategies - Planning for life after medicine
  • Tax optimization - Strategies that worked for you
  • Lessons learned - Both successes and failures

Formatting Requirements

Length

800 - 2,000 words (approximately 4-10 minute read)

Structure

Clear headline, introduction, organized sections, actionable takeaways

Writing Tips

  • Write in first person - share your genuine experience
  • Be specific with numbers when possible (returns, amounts, timelines)
  • Include both the positives and challenges you faced
  • End with actionable takeaways for readers
  • Avoid promotional content or specific investment recommendations

Editorial Process

1
Submit Your Draft

Submit your article through the platform

2
Editorial Review

Our editors review for quality, clarity, and fit (3-5 business days)

3
Feedback & Revisions

You may receive suggestions for improvements

4
Publication

Approved articles are published to our community

What We Don't Accept

  • Promotional content or advertisements
  • Specific stock picks or investment recommendations
  • Previously published content
  • AI-generated content without substantial editing
  • Content violating patient privacy
Disclaimer: All published articles include a standard disclaimer noting that content represents the author's personal views and should not be considered financial advice.
