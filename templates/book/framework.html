The 10B Framework Deep Dive

A comprehensive guide to building lasting wealth as a medical professional. Follow these 10 steps to achieve financial independence.

The 10 Building Blocks to Financial Freedom

1

Build Your Foundation

Establish an emergency fund, understand your cash flow, and create a solid financial baseline before investing.

  • 3-6 months emergency fund
  • Track income and expenses
  • Eliminate high-interest debt
2

Benefit from Tax Advantages

Maximize tax-advantaged accounts and understand physician-specific tax strategies to keep more of what you earn.

  • Max out 401(k) and backdoor Roth
  • HSA as a wealth-building tool
  • Real estate tax benefits
3

Beat Lifestyle Inflation

As your income grows, avoid the trap of proportionally increasing spending. Live like a resident a little longer.

  • Set spending guardrails
  • Automate savings increases
  • Define "enough" for your lifestyle
4

Build Multiple Income Streams

Diversify your income beyond clinical work through investments, side businesses, and passive income sources.

  • Rental real estate income
  • Dividend investing
  • Medical consulting or teaching
5

Balance Risk and Reward

Understand your risk tolerance and create a portfolio that aligns with your timeline and goals.

  • Asset allocation strategy
  • Rebalancing schedule
  • Risk-adjusted returns focus
